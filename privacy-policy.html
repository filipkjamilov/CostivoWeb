Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

Costivo ("we", "our", or "the app") is a cost estimation tool for craftsmen and construction professionals. This policy explains what information the app handles and how it is used.

Data Stored on Your Device

All data you enter in Costivo is stored locally on your device only:

  • Your name and optional company name
  • Materials, labor rates, and pricing you create
  • Job estimates and client names
  • App preferences (currency, profession, language)

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server operated by us. It is deleted when you uninstall the app.

Subscriptions and Payments

Costivo offers an optional paid subscription processed through the App Store. Subscription management is handled by RevenueCat, a third-party service. RevenueCat may collect anonymized purchase and subscription data to validate entitlements. No payment card information is ever accessible to Costivo or RevenueCat — all payments are processed directly by Apple.

RevenueCat's privacy policy is available at revenuecat.com/privacy.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or contact information
  • We do not use advertising or tracking SDKs
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties
  • We do not use analytics tools that track your usage

Children's Privacy

Costivo is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
